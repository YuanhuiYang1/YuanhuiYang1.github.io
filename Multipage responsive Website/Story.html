Justin

Justin is a stunning and quite large GSD boy, around 3 years old and neutered. Justin is not showing any aggression at all and good with all dogs and persons. With humans in particular, Justin is very friendly and affectionate, enjoying being groomed and caressed by his human. Due to his size, his human should possess some physical strength, in order to be able to control this gorgeous dog at all times. Being such a big boy, Justin will profit greatly from basic obedience training. Training will be very effective because Justin is very intelligent, eager to please and listens to his person. He is a dog for a real GSD lover, who would be able to give this diamond in the raw the possibility to shine. With some education and care, this dog is sure to be a show stopper and would attract the admiration of all dog lovers who would meet him.
